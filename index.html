Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my life for generations and never fails to delight

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6-medium sized apples, peeled and sliced
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. In a bowl, mix sugar and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
